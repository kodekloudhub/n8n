XFUSIONCORP - STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (SOP)
Version 3.2 | Effective: January 2025
Classification: Internal Use Only (Mock Data for Training Purposes)

=====================================================================
SECTION 1: CUSTOMER SERVICE - COMPLAINT HANDLING
=====================================================================

1.1 COMPLAINT CLASSIFICATION
All customer complaints must be classified into one of the following categories:
- BILLING: Incorrect charges, disputed amounts, payment issues
- NETWORK: Signal outage, slow speeds, connectivity problems
- ACCOUNT: SIM swap, plan changes, account security
- RETENTION: Churn risk, competitor offers, loyalty concerns
- ENTERPRISE: Business account requests, site surveys, SLA issues

1.2 PRIORITY LEVELS
- P1 (CRITICAL): Complete service outage affecting >1000 customers, security breaches, enterprise SLA violations. Response time: 15 minutes. Resolution target: 4 hours.
- P2 (HIGH): Individual service outage >24 hours, billing disputes >PHP 5000, retention risk (8+ year customers). Response time: 1 hour. Resolution target: 24 hours.
- P3 (MEDIUM): Intermittent issues, standard billing disputes, plan change requests. Response time: 4 hours. Resolution target: 48 hours.
- P4 (LOW): General inquiries, feature requests, positive feedback. Response time: 24 hours. Resolution target: 72 hours.

1.3 ESCALATION MATRIX
- Level 1 (Frontline Agent): Basic troubleshooting, account inquiries, standard requests
- Level 2 (Senior Agent): Complex billing issues, advanced troubleshooting, retention offers
- Level 3 (Team Lead): Escalated complaints, NTC-related issues, social media crises
- Level 4 (Manager): Legal threats, media-covered incidents, VIP enterprise accounts

=====================================================================
SECTION 2: NETWORK OPERATIONS
=====================================================================

2.1 NETWORK ALARM HANDLING
- NORMAL: No action required. Log for reporting.
- WARNING: Monitor for 30 minutes. If condition persists, create maintenance ticket.
- CRITICAL: Immediately notify NOC Team Lead. Create P1 incident. Dispatch field team within 2 hours.

2.2 SITE AVAILABILITY THRESHOLDS
- 5G Sites: Minimum 99.5% availability. Alert if drops below 99%.
- 4G Sites: Minimum 98.0% availability. Alert if drops below 97%.
- 3G/Legacy: Minimum 95.0% availability. Alert if drops below 94%.

2.3 THROUGHPUT STANDARDS
- 5G: Average >200 Mbps, Peak >400 Mbps
- 4G: Average >50 Mbps, Peak >100 Mbps
- Fiber (Residential): Deliver minimum 80% of subscribed speed
- Fiber (Enterprise): Deliver minimum 95% of subscribed speed with SLA guarantee

2.4 INCIDENT RESPONSE PROCEDURE
1. Detect: Automated monitoring or customer report
2. Classify: Determine impact scope and severity
3. Notify: Alert affected teams per escalation matrix
4. Diagnose: Root cause analysis within 2 hours
5. Resolve: Apply fix and verify service restoration
6. Communicate: Update affected customers via SMS/app notification
7. Document: Complete incident report within 24 hours

=====================================================================
SECTION 3: BILLING AND REVENUE
=====================================================================

3.1 BILLING DISPUTE RESOLUTION
- Disputes <PHP 500: Agent can credit immediately with supervisor approval code
- Disputes PHP 500-5000: Requires Team Lead review and approval within 24 hours
- Disputes >PHP 5000: Requires Manager approval and investigation within 48 hours
- All credits must be logged with reason code and reference ticket number

3.2 RETENTION OFFERS (AUTHORIZED)
- Customers 1-3 years: 10% discount for 3 months or free data add-on
- Customers 3-5 years: 15% discount for 6 months or one-tier plan upgrade
- Customers 5-8 years: 20% discount for 6 months or device discount voucher
- Customers 8+ years: Up to 25% discount for 12 months, VIP priority support, or matching competitor offer (requires Manager approval)

3.3 REVENUE REPORTING
- Daily subscriber count reports by 10 AM to Regional Heads
- Weekly revenue flash reports every Monday to Business Unit Heads
- Monthly P&L reports by 5th business day to CFO office
- All reports must include: actuals vs. budget, MoM comparison, YoY comparison

SECTION 4: ENTERPRISE SERVICES
=====================================================================

4.1 ENTERPRISE SITE SURVEY
- Response to survey request: Within 3 business days
- Survey completion: Within 5 business days of site visit
- Proposal delivery: Within 3 business days after survey
- Installation: Within 15 business days after contract signing

4.2 SLA TIERS
- Bronze: 99.0% uptime, 8-hour response, business hours support
- Silver: 99.5% uptime, 4-hour response, extended hours support
- Gold: 99.9% uptime, 2-hour response, 24/7 support with dedicated account manager
- Platinum: 99.95% uptime, 1-hour response, 24/7 NOC with proactive monitoring

=====================================================================
SECTION 5: SECURITY AND FRAUD
=====================================================================

5.1 SIM SWAP - STOLEN PHONE PROCEDURE
1. Verify customer identity using 3 of 5 verification points:
   - Full name and birthdate
   - Last 4 digits of payment method
   - Last 3 calls or texts made
   - Account registration email
   - Security question answer
2. Immediately block the old SIM
3. Flag linked financial services (xCash, banking apps)
4. Issue new SIM with same number within 24 hours
5. Send confirmation via registered email
6. Monitor account for 30 days for suspicious activity

5.2 FRAUD INDICATORS
- Multiple SIM swap requests within 30 days
- Unusual international call patterns
- Sudden spike in data usage (>500% normal)
- Account access from multiple IPs in different regions
- Bill payment from unregistered payment methods
